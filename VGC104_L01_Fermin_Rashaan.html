 The Legend fo Zelda: Twilight Princess was release in North America on November 19, 2006 for the Ninetendo Wii and on December 11 for the Ninetnedo Gamecube.The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess was later on released as a remaster for the Wii U released in North America on March 4, 2016 which came with enhanced visuals and Amiibo support. The game features Link, who in this era is called the hero of twilight, going through a quest to save the land of Hyrule from the twilight which is an alternate dimension of dark creatures. When Link first enters the twilight, his form changes into that of a wolf and accomponied by his new companion Midna, he must defeat the evil Zant and bring light and peace back to this dimension.
